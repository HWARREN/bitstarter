Who are we?

Ever want to announce to the world something special as a shout out on the Internet? A love you have? A milestone birthday for Dad or Grandma? Reached a personal milestone? Did your company get a big contract or have a new product ship that you're proud of?

Celebrate Everything is for you!

Read on for a description and to see how you can get involved in making Celebrate Everything a reality!

Celebrate Everything will allow you to announce to the world anything you desire!

  • We provide you with free templates for your celebration page.
  • With our templates you can have a celebration page on the Internet in an instant.
  • 100% free for a foundation celebration page.
  • A low one-time flat fee for a premium celebration page.
  • We take care of all hosting concerns for your page.
  • Your celebaration page can be anonymous. It's your choice.
  • No need to sign-up, register, or log-in for a foundation celebration page.
  • You can customize a premium celebration page further from the provided templates.

We need you to be involved in the build out of Celebrate Everything!

  • By preordering premium celebration pages, your financial support will allow Celebrate Everything to go live!
  • If our campaign goal is reached, Celebrate Everything will begin build out.
  • No money will be collected unless our campaign goal is reached.
  • All preorders over $40us will receive rewards!

How can you get involved? Press the Preorder button at the top!

Thank you for your support!

FAQ

Does Celebrate Everything currently have a web page?
No. Based on reaching the campaign goal build out would begin. Otherwise, no money will be collected and the site will not be built out. That way the market interest in Celebrate Everything can be determined by the level of preorders before committing resources to build out.
What am I preordering?
You are preordering premium celebration pages for a low pre-build out fee. In addition, for a preorder of $40us or greater, a reward will be offered.
What rewards will be offered?
Rewards will be determined when this campaign site goes live.
Has a domain name been registered?
No. "Celebrate Everything" is a working title. The website and domain name will be determined once build out begins.
